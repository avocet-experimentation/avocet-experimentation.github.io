Given that experiments must be designed to meet the needs of the research problem, it should be surprising to learn that many software experimentation platforms focus on A/B tests, with limited or no support for switchback tests. They also come with other drawbacks, such as limited statistical analysis, selective interoperability with telemetry providers, and vendor lock-in.

In keeping with popular experimentation platforms such as LaunchDarkly, GrowthBook, and Statsig, Avocet leverages feature flagging to define treatments, and software instrumentation to measure the effects of treatments. A few things set Avocet apart:
